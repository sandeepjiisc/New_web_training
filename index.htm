    Vibration energy harvesting:

    This project deals with energy harvesting from the vibration, which is obtained from the mechanical systems
    or even from the ambient environment. It is one of the noble way of scavenging energy from surrounding. The
    energy so produced can be used to power the wireless sensors, many small devices used in hostile
    environmental condition, used to charge batteries and many more. Here attention is concentrated on the
    vibration analysis of the system using Matlab and Ansys softwares. It also includes designing of the
    prototypes. Here the basic element for energy harvestings are piezoelectric crystals.
